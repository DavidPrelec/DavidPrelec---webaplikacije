DJ MAG Pronounces Papaya 2ND BEST CLUB by the sea!


Is there anything better than dancing on the sand, or overlooking the blue ocean? There is something very special about partying outdoors, and on the coast...

"Think it’s just about festivals in Croatia nowadays? Think again. Papaya, situated on Zrce Beach on the island of Pag, is a bona fide Croatian clubbing success story. Each summer season this open-air behemoth in a stunning setting hosts a series of one-off DJ headlined events. Everyone from Jamie Jones, Sven Väth, Justice and Loco Dice to Hardwell, Nicky Romero and W&W have appeared here, ensuring the underground and more commercial sounds are equally well-represented. It is also the home of Hideout and the DJ Mag Beach Festival." - quote by DJ MAG


DJ MAG, one of the most prestige music magazines of today, published their pick of 7 best clubs by the sea and guess what - Papaya club took 2nd place! Nikki beach club (Miami), Lost Beach club (Ecuador), Guaba beach club (Cyprus), Paradise club (Mykonos) and Cacao beach (Bulgary) also made the list.


DJ MAG magazine has over the years become the most famous electronic music magazine, with the special emphasis on their Top 100 DJs and Top 100 Clubs lists. The lists have become the main reference point of every world famous DJ or club and the official acknowledgment of their success in comparison to others around the world.


That's why we proudly present that Papaya club is the 23rd best club of the world according to the DJ MAG Top 100 Clubs list. Zrce beach has become famous for its wild after beach parties, loads of beautiful cheerful people, refreshing cocktails and festivals that feature top DJs of the world.


DJ MAG and Papaya club are preparing the fifth festival in their cooperation at Croatia’s most famous beach Zrce. Check out DJ MAG Beach festival 2015 featured with Showtek, Dannic, VINAI and Blinders.
