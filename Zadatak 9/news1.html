The first massive event of next summer season is hot & ready!


Welcome to the Big Beach Spring Break 2023!

Zrce beach has in the last decade become one of the favorite European spring break destination with thousands of young party people from all over the world and each year we add a few more nationalities to our party list.


Spring break parties held both in Papaya club's pool area during the day and under their fantastic big stage in the night will surely not disappoint you.


You'll come to see that they're not lying when they say there's something special about partying on the island of Pag - hot summer nights, countless adventures, beautiful girls and hot guys, clear Adriatic, great music, DJs & animators creating an atmosphere you'll never forget.


What does the event include? FREE entrance to all club pool parties (every day 15-20h), Best night parties on the beach (every day 23-05h), Famous DJs, Best production, sound and special effects on the beach, Dancers, hostesses, MC.


So prepare yourself for this event which will represent the grand opening of the festival season in club Papaya! Let's kick into the summer of 2023 with style, see you all there!
