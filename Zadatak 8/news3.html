NEW COVID PASS PROTOCOL FROM 01.07.2021.


From 1st of July 2021 Zrce beach is starting a new Covid Pass protocol!

Pursuant to the “Decision on Necessary Epidemiological Measures Restricting Gatherings and Introducing Other Necessary Epidemiological Measures and Recommendations to Prevent the Transmission of COVID-19 Disease through Gatherings” effective from July 1st 2021, catering facilities such as night clubs, night bars, disco bars and disco clubs from the category "Bars" are allowed to work and serve all guests without restrictions on working hours, but only in open spaces and provided that the entrance to their premises is allowed only to persons holding an EU digital COVID certificate (guests, staff, performers, etc.).


Important general info: ● Entry into Zrce beach clubs will be allowed ONLY to guests, staff, performers, etc. who have a digital or paper document that they are vaccinated / negative / had a positive corona test in the last 6 months ● With every mentioned document you will also need to present a valid photo ID card. ● EU approved vaccines are Pfizer, Moderna, AstraZeneca and Janssen ● All other vaccines we are unfortunately not allowed to accept so people need to get an additional PCR or Antigen test


Fully vaccinated guests who have their EU digital COVID certificate Guests who have the EU digital COVID certificate need ti have it downloaded on their mobile phone/tablet or printed out + they need to show their valid photo ID card


Guests that have proof that they had a positive corona test within the last 6 months: Guests who have had corona in the last 6 months need to have legal document that proves it + they need to show their valid photo ID card


Guest with 1 dose of vaccine: Guests who have been vaccinated with 1 dose don't need to be tested additionally if they have a document showing they have been vaccinated more than 22 days prior + they need to show their valid photo ID card
