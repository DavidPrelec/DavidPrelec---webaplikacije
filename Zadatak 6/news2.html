DJ MAG pronounces Papaya 10th best club in the world!


It is a global clubbing powerhouse in terms of the DJs, parties and festivals it books and has helped to spearhead the Croatian clubbing revolution over the last 10 years.

Papaya Club regularly features in the higher echelons of our poll and for good reason: it is a global clubbing powerhouse in terms of the DJs, parties and festivals it books and has helped to spearhead the Croatian clubbing revolution over the last 10 years.


It is of course home to one of the most notable events of the summer, Hideout festival, and is also well known and loved for hosting after-beach pool parties for six hours every day.


Those are always well attended, with on-point residents supplying the sounds as sun, seascapes, drinks and dancing make for a wild afternoon.


There is also a restaurant, plus two pools, a VIP lounge area and regular sunset boat parties to enjoy.


The club turns 20 this year, so expect some extra special treats and surprises and a non-stop party atmosphere all season long.
